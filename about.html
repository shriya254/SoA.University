Siksha ‘O’ Anusandhan University (SOA) is a deemed to be a university. It was established in 2007 & is located in Odisha. The university is approved by the UGC and accredited by NAAC, ABET, NBA. It is recognised by the Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (SIRO). The university offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral level courses with around 1,300 faculty members & nine departments. SOA was ranked 20th in the country by the NIRF in 2020 occupying first place in Odisha. The university provides infrastructure and facilities like a sports complex, cafeteria, Research Laboratories, and many more. SOA has 42 departmental libraries.
